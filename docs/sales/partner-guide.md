## Farsight Partnership Deep Dive

**Why Farsight?**
- Leading Scottish portfolio management platform
- 1,200+ wealth manager clients managing £80B+ AUM
- Clients need FCA compliance tools (perfect LOKI fit)
- Trusted brand in wealth management community

**Integration Plan:**

**Q1 2026:**
- API integration (LOKI as microservice within Farsight platform)
- Beta testing with 50 Farsight clients
- Co-marketing materials development

**Q2 2026:**
- General availability to all Farsight clients
- Pricing embedded in Farsight premium tier (£49/month per firm)
- Joint webinar series on FCA Consumer Duty compliance

**Q3-Q4 2026:**
- Advanced features: bulk validation, compliance dashboard
- Case studies and success metrics
- Expand to Farsight's international clients

**Revenue Model:**
```
Farsight client pays: £49/month
├── Farsight receives: £34.30 (70%)
└── Highland AI receives: £14.70 (30%)

Year 1: 300 clients × £14.70 × 12 = £53K to Highland AI
Year 2: 600 clients × £14.70 × 12 = £106K
Year 3: 900 clients × £14.70 × 12 = £158K
```

**Benefits to Farsight:**
- Competitive differentiation (only platform with compliance)
- Premium tier upsell opportunity
- Client retention (compliance tool creates stickiness)
- FCA relationship credibility

**Benefits to Highland AI:**
- Access to 1,200 qualified wealth manager leads
- Brand validation (Farsight endorsement)
- Recurring revenue with minimal CAC
- Case studies and proof points

**Joint Success Metrics:**
- Target: 75% adoption by Year 3 (900 of 1,200 clients)
- NPS score: >60
- Support ticket volume: <1% of users/month
- Upsell rate: 10% to LOKI direct (API access, Enterprise features)
