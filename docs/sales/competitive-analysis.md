## Competitive Response Scenarios

### Scenario 1: BrightHR Builds FCA Module

**Timeline:** 18-24 months (build AI team, FCA expertise, testing)
**Impact on LOKI:** Medium (BrightHR has distribution advantage)
**Mitigation:**
- Expand to modules 6-10 (EU MDR, ISO, etc.) before BrightHR catches up
- Lock in Farsight + other partnerships (switching costs)
- Build data moat (1,000+ customers = better training data)

### Scenario 2: LawGeex Expands UK Coverage

**Timeline:** 12-18 months (hire UK compliance experts, build rules)
**Impact on LOKI:** Low (LawGeex focused on US, LOKI has UK first-mover)
**Mitigation:**
- Double down on UK-specific features (Scottish law, MTD, Consumer Duty)
- Partner with UK wealth managers, accounting firms (UK distribution)
- UK-based support and regulatory updates (competitive advantage)

### Scenario 3: Big Tech Enters (Microsoft, Thomson Reuters)

**Timeline:** 24-36 months (slow-moving enterprises)
**Impact on LOKI:** High (brand, distribution, resources)
**Mitigation:**
- Acquisition target (LOKI would be strategic buy for big tech)
- Niche specialization (deep compliance vs. broad legal tech)
- Agility (ship features 10x faster than enterprise)

### Scenario 4: Peninsula Acquires a Tech Competitor

**Timeline:** 6-12 months (M&A market active in RegTech)
**Impact on LOKI:** Medium (Peninsula gets tech capability)
**Mitigation:**
- Be the acquisition target (Highland AI + Peninsula merger?)
- Focus on self-serve (Peninsula's phone model is hard to change)
- API/integration play (LOKI becomes middleware for all players)
