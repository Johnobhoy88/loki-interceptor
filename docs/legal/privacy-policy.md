## 7. Your Data Protection Rights (UK GDPR)

### 7.1 Right to Access (Article 15)

Request copy of your personal data.
- **How:** Email privacy@highlandai.com
- **Response Time:** 30 days
- **Fee:** Free (first request), £10 for subsequent requests within 6 months

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **How:** Update in portal.loki-compliance.com or email privacy@highlandai.com
- **Response Time:** 72 hours

### 7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Article 17)

Request deletion of your data.
- **How:** Email privacy@highlandai.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
- **Response Time:** 30 days
- **Limitations:** Cannot delete if needed for legal obligations (e.g., billing records)

### 7.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

Limit how we process your data.
- **How:** Email privacy@highlandai.com
- **Effect:** Data stored but not processed (Service may be limited)

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

Receive your data in machine-readable format.
- **How:** Email privacy@highlandai.com
- **Format:** JSON export of account data, audit logs, validation results

### 7.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

Object to processing under legitimate interest.
- **How:** Email privacy@highlandai.com
- **Effect:** We stop processing unless compelling legitimate grounds

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for marketing emails or analytics.
- **Marketing:** Click "Unsubscribe" in any email
- **Analytics:** Disable in portal.loki-compliance.com > Settings > Privacy

### 7.8 Right to Complain

Lodge complaint with supervisory authority.
- **UK ICO:** https://ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/
- **Phone:** 0303 123 1113
- **Address:** Information Commissioner's Office, Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire, SK9 5AF

## 8. Data Security

### 8.1 Technical Measures

**Encryption:**
- AES-256 encryption at rest (database, backups)
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit (API, web)
- Password hashing (bcrypt, salted)

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for employees
- Principle of least privilege

**Infrastructure:**
- AWS SOC 2 Type II certified data centers
- Firewall protection
- DDoS mitigation (Cloudflare)
- Intrusion detection systems (IDS)

**Monitoring:**
- 24/7 automated monitoring
- Security incident response plan
- Annual penetration testing

### 8.2 Organizational Measures

- Employee data protection training (annual)
- Background checks for employees with data access
- Data processing agreements with all vendors
- Incident response plan (breach notification within 72 hours)

### 8.3 Data Breach Response

If personal data breach occurs:
1. **Containment:** Immediately isolate affected systems
2. **Investigation:** Determine scope, cause, affected data
3. **Notification:**
   - ICO: Within 72 hours (if high risk)
   - Affected individuals: Without undue delay (if high risk)
   - Details: Nature of breach, likely consequences, measures taken
4. **Remediation:** Fix vulnerability, prevent recurrence

**Contact for Security Issues:** security@highlandai.com
