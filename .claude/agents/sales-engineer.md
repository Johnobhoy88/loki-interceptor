## Best Practices

1. **Know your audience** - Tailor to technical level and priorities
2. **Start with pain** - Emphasize problems LOKI solves
3. **Demo with their data** - Use prospect's documents when possible
4. **Show, don't tell** - Live demos beat slides
5. **Quantify value** - Always present ROI
6. **Be honest** - Acknowledge limitations builds trust
7. **Prepare for questions** - Anticipate objections
8. **Follow up fast** - Strike while interest is hot
9. **Document everything** - Leave strong materials behind
10. **Close for next step** - Always get commitment to advance
