# LOKI Interceptor - Go-To-Market Strategy
**Partner**: Farsight Digital
**Version**: 1.0
**Date**: November 2025

---

## Executive Summary

LOKI Interceptor's go-to-market strategy focuses on a phased, partner-led approach targeting UK financial services SMEs with an acute compliance pain point following FCA Consumer Duty implementation. The strategy leverages Farsight Digital's established relationships and compliance expertise to achieve 50 customers in Year 1, scaling to 1,000 customers by Year 5.

**Core GTM Pillars:**
1. Partner-led customer acquisition (Farsight Digital primary)
2. Thought leadership & content marketing
3. Product-led growth (free trial → paid conversion)
4. Community building (compliance officer network)

**Year 1 Target**: 50 customers, £2.6M ARR
**Primary Channel**: Farsight Digital partnership (60% of acquisitions)

### Sales Methodology

**MEDDIC Framework:**
- **Metrics**: What's the measurable impact? (time saved, cost reduction)
- **Economic Buyer**: Who controls the budget?
- **Decision Criteria**: What are they evaluating? (accuracy, ease of use, ROI)
- **Decision Process**: Who's involved? Timeline?
- **Identify Pain**: What's the specific compliance pain point?
- **Champion**: Who internally is advocating for LOKI?

**Sales Stages:**
1. **Discovery** (Week 1)
   - Understand compliance workflow
   - Identify pain points (manual review time, error risk, cost)
   - Quantify current state (documents/month, FTE cost, penalty risk)

2. **Demo** (Week 2)
   - Live product demo using their document examples
   - Show violation detection + autocorrection
   - Highlight relevant gates (FCA, GDPR, etc.)

3. **Trial** (Week 3-4)
   - 14-day free trial (Starter/Professional)
   - Weekly check-in calls
   - Usage monitoring (documents processed, gates triggered)

4. **Proposal** (Week 5)
   - Custom pricing proposal
   - ROI calculation
   - Implementation plan

5. **Negotiation & Close** (Week 6-8)
   - Address objections (security, integration, pricing)
   - Contracts & legal review
   - Close deal

**Average Sales Cycle:**
- Starter: 2 weeks
- Professional: 6 weeks
- Enterprise: 10 weeks

## Risk Mitigation

### GTM Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Slow Partner Ramp**
- Mitigation: Invest in partner enablement, co-selling in first 6 months
- Backup: Increase inbound marketing budget (+£10k/month)

**Risk 2: Long Enterprise Sales Cycles**
- Mitigation: Focus on Professional tier (6-week cycles) in Year 1
- Backup: Offer Starter tier to get foot in door, upsell later

**Risk 3: Low Trial Conversion**
- Mitigation: Hands-on onboarding, weekly check-in calls during trial
- Backup: Extend trial to 30 days, offer guided proof of concept

**Risk 4: Competitor Response**
- Mitigation: Build deep moat (141 rules, autocorrection IP, partner network)
- Backup: Accelerate product development, lock in customers with annual contracts
