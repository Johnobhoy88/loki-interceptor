## Executive Summary

Scaling LOKI Interceptor from production-ready prototype to £52M ARR enterprise requires strategic investment in engineering, sales, operations, and infrastructure. This document outlines phased resource requirements aligned with revenue milestones, totaling £7.5M investment over 5 years with a focus on sustainable, capital-efficient growth.

**Investment Breakdown:**
- **Seed (Year 1)**: £500k - Product completion, pilot customers, foundation team (3 FTE)
- **Series A (Year 2)**: £2M - Sales team scaling, marketing, infrastructure (12 FTE total)
- **Series B (Year 3)**: £5M - International expansion, product development (25 FTE total)

**Key Resource Needs:**
1. Development team (7 engineers by Year 3)
2. Sales & marketing (12 FTE by Year 3)
3. Customer success & support (4 FTE by Year 3)
4. Cloud infrastructure (AWS/Azure)
5. AI costs (Claude API)

## Risk Mitigation

### Resource Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Key Engineering Hire Delay**
- Mitigation: Start recruiting in Month -1 (before funding close)
- Backup: Use contractors for short-term gaps (£80-£120/hour)

**Risk 2: Claude API Cost Overrun**
- Mitigation: Implement aggressive caching (target: 50% hit rate)
- Backup: Negotiate volume discounts with Anthropic (commit to £200k/year spend)

**Risk 3: Sales Team Underperformance**
- Mitigation: Invest in enablement, clear sales playbook, partner support
- Backup: Increase inbound marketing spend to compensate

**Risk 4: Customer Support Overload**
- Mitigation: Build comprehensive knowledge base, video tutorials
- Backup: Contract with support outsourcing firm for overflow
