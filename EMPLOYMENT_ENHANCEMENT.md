### Module Descriptions:

#### 1. Employment Rights Act 2023 Checker (era_2023.py)
**Key Provisions:**
- Day-one flexible working rights (removed 26-week qualifying period)
- Carer's leave (1 week unpaid per year, day one right)
- Neonatal care leave (12 weeks per parent)
- Tips and gratuities protection (fair allocation)
- Fire and rehire restrictions
- Enhanced pregnancy redundancy protection

**Legal Citations:**
- Employment Relations (Flexible Working) Act 2023
- Neonatal Care Leave and Pay Act 2023
- Employment (Allocation of Tips) Act 2023

#### 2. Flexible Working Rights Validator (flexible_working.py)
**Compliance Checks:**
- Day one right (no qualifying period post-2023)
- Two requests per 12 months
- Two-month decision deadline
- Eight statutory business reasons for refusal
- Appeal rights
- Meeting and consultation requirements

**Legal Source:** Employment Rights Act 1996 s.80F (as amended 2023)

#### 3. Worker Status Determination (worker_status.py)
**Three-Category Assessment:**
1. **Employee** - Full employment rights
   - Mutuality of obligation
   - Personal service
   - Control
   - Integration
   - Exclusivity

2. **Worker** (Limb b) - Intermediate rights
   - Contract for personal services
   - Not client/customer of business
   - Limited substitution

3. **Self-Employed** - Limited protections
   - Business on own account
   - Business risk
   - Multiple clients
   - Unfettered substitution
   - Own equipment

**Case Law Applied:**
- Autoclenz v Belcher [2011] UKSC 41 (reality test)
- Uber v Aslam [2021] UKSC 5 (worker status)
- Pimlico Plumbers v Smith [2018] UKSC 29 (substitution)
- Ready Mixed Concrete v Minister of Pensions [1968] (three-stage test)

**Rights Matrix:**
- Employee: Unfair dismissal, redundancy pay, statutory notice, TUPE, full protection
- Worker: NMW, holiday pay, rest breaks, whistleblowing, discrimination
- Self-employed: H&S, discrimination (if personally providing services)

#### 4. Unfair Dismissal Protection (unfair_dismissal.py)
**Fair Reasons (s.98 ERA):**
- Capability/qualifications
- Conduct
- Redundancy
- Statutory bar (illegality)
- Some other substantial reason (SOSR)

**Automatically Unfair Reasons:**
- Pregnancy/maternity
- Whistleblowing (PIDA)
- Trade union membership/activities
- Health and safety
- Flexible working request
- Asserting statutory rights

**ACAS Code Requirements:**
- Investigation
- Meeting with employee
- Right to be accompanied
- Written decision with reasons
- Appeal right
- **Penalty:** Up to 25% uplift for non-compliance

#### 5. Equality Act Compliance (equality.py)
**9 Protected Characteristics (s.4):**
1. Age
2. Disability
3. Gender reassignment
4. Marriage and civil partnership
5. Pregnancy and maternity
6. Race
7. Religion or belief
8. Sex
9. Sexual orientation

**Forms of Discrimination:**
- Direct discrimination (s.13)
- Indirect discrimination (s.19)
- Harassment (s.26)
- Victimisation (s.27)

**Penalty:** Unlimited compensation

#### 6. Reasonable Adjustments (adjustments.py)
**Three Duties (s.20 Equality Act):**
1. Changing provisions, criteria, practices
2. Altering physical features
3. Providing auxiliary aids

**Requirements:**
- Anticipatory duty
- Individual assessment
- Consultation with disabled person
- Examples: flexible hours, equipment, physical access, job redesign

**Pre-Employment Health Questions:**
- Prohibited except for: reasonable adjustments, monitoring, positive action (s.60)

#### 7. Whistleblowing (PIDA) Compliance (whistleblowing.py)
**Six Qualifying Disclosures (s.43B ERA):**
1. Criminal offence
2. Breach of legal obligation
3. Miscarriage of justice
4. Health and safety danger
5. Environmental damage
6. Cover-up of any of the above

**Requirements:**
- Reasonable belief
- Public interest test
- Internal procedure (designated person)
- External prescribed persons (FCA, HSE, ICO, etc.)

**Protection:**
- No detriment (s.47B)
- Automatic unfair dismissal if dismissed (s.103A)
- Unlimited compensation

#### 8. TUPE Transfer Validator (tupe.py)
**TUPE 2006 Requirements:**
- Automatic transfer of employees (reg.4)
- Protection of terms and conditions
- No variation solely due to transfer
- Consultation (minimum 28 days before, reg.13)
- Employee Liability Information (ELI) - 28 days before (reg.11)
- Dismissal automatically unfair if sole reason is transfer (reg.7)

**ETO Defense:**
- Economic, technical, or organisational reason entailing changes in workforce

**Penalties:**
- Up to 13 weeks' pay per employee for consultation failures
- Unfair dismissal claims
- Breach of contract for varied terms

#### 9. Settlement Agreement Compliance (settlements.py)
**s.203 ERA 1996 Requirements (7 conditions):**
1. In writing
2. Relates to particular complaint or proceedings
3. Independent adviser
4. Adviser identified in agreement
5. Adviser has professional indemnity insurance
6. Employee received independent legal advice
7. Conditions of agreement satisfied

**Relevant Independent Advisers:**
- Qualified solicitor
- Barrister
- Legal executive (Fellow of CILEX)
- Trade union officer/official
- Advice centre worker

**Prohibited Waivers:**
- Personal injury claims
- Future rights
- Pension rights (unless proper advice)

**Penalty:** Agreement void and unenforceable if requirements not met
